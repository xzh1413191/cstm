# MIFB Sales Phone Script - 2026-07-06

Public page:

https://xzh1413191.github.io/cstm/mifb-phone-script-2026-07-06/

Purpose:

- Help sales staff call T0-T2 customers with existing phone numbers.
- Goal is to get the direct WhatsApp or email of the packaging/purchasing person in charge.
- Secondary goal is to book a MIFB booth meeting or office/factory visit.

Important:

- Do not ask for a "private contact" directly. Use "direct WhatsApp" or "direct email of the packaging/purchasing person in charge".
- Keep the call short. The first call is for routing and permission to send sample photos, not for long product explanation.
