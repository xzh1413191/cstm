MIFB 电话开发话术表

目标：拿到采购/包装负责人直连 WhatsApp 或邮箱，并预约 MIFB 展位 2709 或客户办公室/工厂见面。

打法：电话里不要长篇介绍产品。先找 packaging / purchasing person in charge；拿到 direct WhatsApp/email；再发样品图和约见。不要说 private contact，改说 direct WhatsApp/direct email。

1 开场找负责人前台/总机Hi, this is Kevin from HUASUE Mould Plastic. We manufacture food plastic packaging for cookies, snacks, chocolate and bakery products. May I speak with the person in charge of packaging or purchasing?先别讲太多产品。目标是转采购/包装负责人。转接采购/包装负责人
2 对方问你是谁前台或采购We are a plastic packaging manufacturer. We have PP tubs, tamper-evident boxes, PET jars and clear boxes. I will be in Malaysia for MIFB, booth 2709, and I hope to show suitable samples to your team.一句话说清楚你是谁、做什么、为什么现在联系。让对方知道不是消费者投诉/普通推销
3 要采购直连前台/行政Could I have the direct WhatsApp or email of the packaging/purchasing person in charge? I can send product photos first, so they can decide whether it is worth a short meeting.不要说 private contact，用 direct WhatsApp/direct email 更自然。拿到采购WhatsApp或邮箱
4 对方不方便给联系方式前台/行政No problem. Could you help me forward a short message to them? May I know the best email address for packaging or purchasing enquiries?退一步拿部门邮箱或让对方转发。至少拿到有效邮箱
5 已转采购后采购/包装负责人Hi, I am Kevin from HUASUE. I noticed your company has products that may use tubs, jars or retail snack packaging. We make PP and PET food packaging, including T/J tubs and tamper-evident boxes. May I send you a few sample photos by WhatsApp?采购接电话后，第一目标不是约见，是获得允许发图。获得WhatsApp发图许可
6 对方问价格采购Price depends on size, material, printing and order quantity. Before quoting, I would like to understand your current packaging size and annual or monthly usage. If the product matches, I can bring samples during MIFB.不要一上来报价。先问尺寸和用量。获取SKU/尺寸/用量
7 对方说已有供应商采购Understood. Many factories already have suppliers. We are not asking you to change immediately. I would like to show an alternative option for new products, seasonal packs or backup supply. If useful, we can meet for 10 minutes during MIFB.降低防御：不是马上替换现有供应商。约短会或获得后续机会
8 对方说发邮件采购/前台Sure. Could you give me the best email for packaging or purchasing? After I send it, may I also have a WhatsApp number for quick sample confirmation?发邮件不是结束，要顺手要WhatsApp。邮箱 + WhatsApp
9 约展位见面采购/老板I will be at MIFB 2026, KLCC, booth 2709 from July 15 to 17. Would it be convenient for your purchasing or product team to visit our booth? I can prepare the most relevant samples for your products.展会期间优先让对方来展位。确认展位拜访日期/时间/人数
10 约我去拜访采购/老板I will be in Malaysia from July 11 to July 18. If your team is not visiting MIFB, I can also visit your office or factory before or after the exhibition, depending on your schedule.给对方两个选择：来展位，或你去拜访。拿地址、时间、联系人
11 对方没兴趣采购May I ask if your current products mainly use soft packs, paper boxes, or plastic tubs/jars? I can mark it correctly and avoid disturbing you if it is not suitable.即使没兴趣，也收集包装类型，避免下次浪费时间。获得不适配原因
12 结束确认任何对象Thank you. I will send the sample photos and company information shortly. May I confirm the contact person, WhatsApp/email, and the best time to follow up?电话最后必须复述信息。确认联系人和下一步
